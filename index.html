    What Is Tithing?

    It says in Leviticus 27:30-34 "A tithe of everything from the land, whether grain from the soil or fruit from the trees, belongs to the Lord; it is holy to the Lord."
    Tithing is mentioned multiple times throughout the bible, both Old and New Testament. The act of tithing is giving 10% of our income to show our acknowledgement that all we own belongs to the Lord. Tithing shows our love and commitment to the Lord of what he has done for us at the Cross.
    Offerings however, are different from tithing. After giving your 10% anything else that comes after that is considered offerings. Offerings aren't commanded by God, rather they come from us giving generously.
    Tithes & Offerings are used to help with our mission of saving souls and making disciples. Interested in helping us plant churches in our communities? We'd love to connect with you!
