Our Mission

Just like Jesus told us in Matthew 28:19-20, our mission is to save souls and make disiciples across all nations. Here at Family Christian Church, we strive to fulfill this command by Jesus through a process called Every Family For Jesus .
Every Family For Jesus is a 10 week process dedicated towards bringing Jesus into your family and household. Interested in starting Every Family For Jesus for your family? Contact us today and we can get started.
